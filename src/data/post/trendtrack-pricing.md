## TrendTrack Starter Plan Pricing ($59/month)

### What's Included in the Starter Plan

The Starter plan is the entry point for TrendTrack pricing. For $59 per month (or $47.20 with a 20% promo code), you get:

- **Trending Shops** — Browse 10,000+ Shopify stores with traffic, growth, and ad data
- **Smart Segments** — Save filter combinations for repeatable research workflows
- **Full store analytics** — Traffic estimates, top products, tech stack detection, pixel configuration
- **Chrome Extension** — All seven built-in analysis tools, free forever
- **2 BrandTracker slots** — Monitor up to 2 competitors with daily updates
- **Unlimited ad saves and store favorites**

The Starter plan gives you genuine research capability. You can discover trending stores, analyze their tech stacks, track two key competitors, and build a repeatable research workflow. For a solo dropshipper or a new brand owner validating their first product, this is enough to get started.

### Starter Plan Limitations

The two constraints that matter most:

1. **BrandTracker cap of 2** — If you compete in a niche with more than two direct competitors (most niches do), you will outgrow this limit quickly. BrandTracker is where competitive intelligence compounds over time, and two slots is restrictive.

2. **No Trending Ads access** — The Starter plan does not include the full Trending Ads tab. You can see ads through store profiles, but you cannot search the ad library independently with advanced filters. This means you miss the ability to filter ads by format, duration, spend, and category — which is where most product discovery happens.

#### When to Choose Starter

Choose Starter if:
- You are a solo operator in your first 3–6 months of e-commerce
- You have identified fewer than 3 direct competitors to track
- Your monthly ad spend is under $5,000
- You are still validating whether TrendTrack's data quality meets your standards

Upgrade to Pro when you find yourself wanting to track more than 2 competitors or needing independent ad library search.

### Can I change plans anytime?

Yes. TrendTrack allows plan upgrades and downgrades at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately with a prorated charge. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

### Does TrendTrack pricing include VAT?

TrendTrack pricing is displayed in USD and does not include VAT or local taxes. Depending on your country, VAT may be added at checkout. EU customers should expect a VAT charge on top of the listed price.

### How does annual billing work with TrendTrack pricing?

When you select annual billing, TrendTrack charges the full year upfront at a 30% discount. You can still apply a promo code on top of the annual price for additional savings. The promo code discount applies to the already-reduced annual total, not the monthly sticker price.

### Is there a TrendTrack Enterprise plan?

TrendTrack offers custom Enterprise pricing for large organizations needing API contracts, dedicated account management, and unlimited usage. Contact TrendTrack directly for Enterprise pricing quotes. Most users will not need this — the Business plan covers teams up to 4 users with unlimited brand tracking.
